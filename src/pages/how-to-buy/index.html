To Get Your Dream Cars From Japanese Auction to Anywhere

Signup

To start the procedure, you need to sign up on our website. There is an easy signup option by which you can do it at a glance. We do not take any charge for signing up on our website. You just need to share your required information and confirm the account from your email.

Bidding

You are open to select any vehicle from our list and also can set your bidding range. After that, we will bid on your selected vehicle from the auction and win it for you. The good news is, you do not need to pay any amount if the bid, unfortunately, gets unsuccessful.

Auction Result

From our member area, you will be notified of the result of the auction. Moreover, we will inform you of the result by email. When the bid will be successful, we will provide you the invoice containing the CIF or FOB amount of your selected vehicle.

Make Payment

KM Auto Export offers you the most reliable and flexible payment system. In this option, you have to make the payment as per the invoice sent.

Shipment & Documentation

This process will start instantly when we receive your payment and confirmation. Whenever we receive your money, we will ship your car in your desired destination. We will send you all the papers through DHL.

Visit & Contact With Us

From anywhere you can buy your desired cars. We are providing our services all over the world with our extensive experiences in the automotive field.
