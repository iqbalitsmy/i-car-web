Overview

KM Auto Export is a reputed automobile trading company located in Toyoma, Japan. From anywhere you can buy your desired cars. We are providing our services all over the world with our extensive experiences in the automotive field. We have gained a place in the heart of our customers by providing quality service with outstanding quality used vehicles. Flexible payment method is another key point for our customers to come back to us in a repetitive manner. We have a very suitable payment plan for our customers to ensure maximum clarity and swift transaction. We always chose the best shipping companies to export our vehicles to our respective clients.

Our Mission

The first mission of KM Auto Export is to build a long-lasting relationship with its customers by focusing on quality service and providing high-quality used cars at a reasonable price.

Our Vision

KM Auto Export has a vision of assisting car dealers, brokers, and individuals all over the world to find the best auction cars.
