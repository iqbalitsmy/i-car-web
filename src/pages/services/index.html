Professional Vehicle Inspection

Every vehicle undergoes comprehensive multi-point inspection by certified technicians. We check engine performance, transmission, brakes, suspension, electrical systems, and body condition. Detailed inspection reports with photos are provided to ensure complete transparency about vehicle condition and history.

Complete Export Documentation

We handle all export documentation including export certificates, bill of lading, commercial invoices, and customs declarations. Our experienced team ensures all paperwork is accurate and compliant with international shipping regulations to avoid delays at customs.

Reliable Worldwide Shipping

We offer flexible shipping options including container shipping and RoRo (Roll-on/Roll-off) services to over 100 countries. Our partnership with major shipping lines ensures competitive rates, reliable schedules, and safe transportation of your vehicle with real-time tracking updates.

Comprehensive Marine Insurance

Protect your investment with our comprehensive marine insurance coverage. We offer full coverage against damages, theft, and total loss during transit. Our insurance partners provide competitive rates and quick claim processing to ensure your peace of mind throughout the shipping process.

Port Clearance Assistance

Our local agents provide guidance and support for customs clearance at destination ports. We assist with required documentation, duty calculations, and liaison with customs authorities. Our network of trusted agents ensures smooth clearance process and timely vehicle release.

Flexible Finance Solutions

We offer flexible payment options including installment plans, letters of credit, and trade financing solutions. Our financial partners provide competitive rates and terms tailored to your needs. We make vehicle ownership accessible with transparent pricing and no hidden charges.

24/7 Customer Support

Our dedicated customer support team is available round the clock to assist you. Whether you need updates on your order, shipping status, or technical assistance, our multilingual support team provides prompt and professional service via phone, email, and live chat.

Quality Assurance & Warranty

We stand behind the quality of our vehicles with comprehensive warranty options. All vehicles come with detailed condition reports and we offer extended warranty programs for major components. Our commitment to quality ensures your satisfaction and long-term reliability of your purchase.
